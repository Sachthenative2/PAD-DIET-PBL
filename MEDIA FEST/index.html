Welcome to ALL About Paleo Diet

The Paleo Diet is a diet that consists of foods that can be either hunted or gathered. This can therefore help reduce the amount of both unneeded fats as well as artificial fats in the body.

Insights



The diet can help people who are currently overweight lose weight very quickly, this diet, with excersise, can help people lose weight very quickly.

This diet is also called the 'cave man diet' because this was the diet said pre-historic humans ate.
